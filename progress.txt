## Codebase Patterns
- `./gradlew compileDebugKotlin --rerun` with grep for "warning:.*deprecat" is the reliable way to enumerate real Kotlin-compiler deprecation warnings across app/src/main (gradle's own "Deprecated Gradle features" noise is unrelated and always present - filter it out).
- `./gradlew ktlintFormat` auto-fixes ktlint line-wrap violations introduced by a manual edit faster than hand-formatting.
- Check a new AndroidX package actually ships in the *currently pinned* dependency version before doing a bare import swap for a "moved to package X" deprecation - `jar tf` on the cached transformed jar under ~/.gradle/caches confirms without bumping the version.
